01 — Atelier

We are the contrast.

Between what this building was
and what it is now.

CONTRASTE ATELIER was built inside an old mechanic's workshop on the industrial edge of Tulum. Today, seven workshops share that space — metalwork, woodworking, laser engraving. We arrived to make jewelry. We stayed to make a point.

Not minimalism for its own sake. Not luxury for status. Intentional things, made by hand, in an unlikely place. Raw concrete. Silver. Fire. The contrast is the point.

04 — Classes

Make your own ring.

You choose a design from our collection of wax models. We cast it in silver — or the metal you want. No experience needed. You leave with a ring.

The Wax Program

Choose a design.
Take your ring.

01
Choose a wax model from our collection of designs — all hand-carved in our atelier. Every design is unique.
02
Pick your metal — sterling silver, oxidized silver, gold-plated. Add a stone from our inventory if you want.
03
We cast it using the lost-wax process. Ready in 48–72 hours. Pickup or we ship anywhere in Mexico.
The design is already done.
The price reflects that.

Because you're choosing from existing wax models rather than commissioning a custom design from scratch, you pay significantly less — while still getting a one-of-one cast piece in real silver.
