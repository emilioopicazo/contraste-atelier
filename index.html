La Veleta · Tulum

CONTRASTE ATELIER is a jewelry workshop and creative studio built around process, material and the hand.

We work with wax, silver, fire and tools to make pieces from scratch — and we open that process to anyone who wants to experience jewelry from the inside. The result isn't only an object. It's the time, heat and decisions that built it.

  • 01A real workshop in La Veleta, Tulum — not a tourist activity.
  • 02Private sessions or small groups, by reservation.
  • 03Artisanal process: wax modeling, lost-wax, handmade pieces.
  • 04No previous experience required — the workshop guides you.
